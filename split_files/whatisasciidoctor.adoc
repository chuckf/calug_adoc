
== What is Asciidoctor?

Asciidoctor is a post processor for the Asciidoc markup language

It converts Asciidoc documents to other formats, directly or indirectly, such as those in this [incomplete] list:

** HTML(5)
** PDF
** Docbook
** manpage
** mobi
** epub3
** slides
